Formulas & Theory

Mathematical Formulas

Steam Economy
SE = 2.2 + (Tsteam - 60) / 100 × 0.8
Where: SE = Steam Economy, Tsteam = Steam Temperature (°C)
Heat Transfer Rate
Q = (ṁfeed × Cp × ΔT) / 3600
Where: Q = Heat Transfer Rate (kW), ṁfeed = Feed Rate (kg/hr), Cp = Specific Heat (4.18 kJ/kg·K)
Water Removal & Concentration
Wremoval = 0.70 + (Tsteam - 100) / 500
Cfinal = Cinitial / (1 - Wremoval)
Where: Wremoval = Water Removal Rate, Cfinal = Final Concentration
